> Phone-based soil analysis. Crop recommendations. Fertilizer optimization. Carbon credit estimation.

## The Solution

**SoilAnalyzer** — Smartphone color/texture analysis for approximate NPK and organic matter\n**CropRecommender** — Best crops for soil type, climate zone, and market prices\n**FertilizerOptimizer** — Minimize cost and environmental runoff\n**PestWarning** — Early detection from satellite imagery and weather\n**CarbonEstimator** — Sequestration calculations for carbon credit programs
